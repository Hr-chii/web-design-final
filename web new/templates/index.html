購買可重複使用的物品 Purchase Reusable Items

現代生活中充斥著一次性用品，如塑膠袋、吸管和餐盒，不僅浪費資源，還對環境造成嚴重汙染。 Modern life is filled with disposable items like plastic bags, straws, and food containers, which not only waste resources but also cause severe environmental pollution.

攜帶環保購物袋，拒絕塑膠袋 Bring reusable shopping bags, say no to plastic bags
使用不鏽鋼吸管、可清洗的布巾 Use stainless steel straws and washable cloth napkins
選擇耐用的水瓶、保鮮盒 Choose durable water bottles and food containers
